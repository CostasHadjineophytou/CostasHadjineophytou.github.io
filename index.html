About

I am a software engineer with a keen interest in AI and machine learning. I hold an MSc in Computer Science from UCL, where I graduated with distinction, and a BSc in Mathematics from King's College London, achieving first-class honours.


During my time at UCL, I delved into modules such as Algorithmics, Machine Learning, Computer Architecture & Operating Systems, Database Fundamentals, Functional Programming, Python Programming, Software Engineering and App Engineering. These courses provided me with a comprehensive understanding of both theoretical concepts and practical applications.


My professional journey has involved working on a variety of projects, ranging from AI model integration to full-stack web development. I have collaborated with teams to create software solutions that are both innovative and user-friendly, always striving to enhance the user experience.


Outside of work, I am passionate about continuous learning and staying updated with the latest technological advancements. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and am excited to contribute to this field.
